Welcome to a Futuristic Cityscape!

This page shows images of futuristic cities from sci-fi movies in different time. While making this page I came accross a fine art page by ArtStation.

Blade Runner (1982) & Blade Runner 2049 (2017)

The original Blade Runner was filmed in Los Angeles while the second movie filmed after thirty years in Hungary. In the first movie, the film was set in a dystopian future Los Angeles of 2019, in which synthetic humans known as replicants are bio-engineered by the powerful corporation to work at space colonies. In the second movie, LAPD Officer, unearths a long-buried secret that has the potential to plunge what’s left of society into chaos. These movies have some futuristics flying cars and synthetic humans with some interesting cityscapes.

Tomorrowland (2015)

Tomorrowland was filmed in many countries, such as British Columbia, Vancouver, Canada and Florida, USA. Tomorrowland is a futuristic cityscape, which is only accessible by special people who have an orange pin with a big blue T on it. The girl in the movie finds that pin and whenever she touches it, the pin transports her to Tomorrowland. The land is full of adventures with so many robots, people traveling using jetpack and playing around in the air.

Jupitor Ascending (2015)

This movie was filmed in multiple countries: London, England, Spain, Czech Republic, and Chicago. This movie includes many different planets including Earth. One of the planet in the film was Orus, which is located in the Canbulum system. It is known for the birthplace of humanity and the capital of the Intergalactic Commonwealth and the oldest civilization in the universe. This planet has very futuristic floating buildings and people have hovering powers.

Allegiant (2016)

Allegiant was filmed in Atlanta, Georgia. The group escapes Chicago and passes the wall that was keeping all the people who have damaged genes. The wall was intercepted by soldiers weaponry who take the group to the Bureau of Genetic Welfare, a city of advanced technology that is hidden behind a cloaking shield.

Maze Runner: The Death Cure (2018)

The Death Cure is a dystopian sci-fi which was filmed in Cape Town, South Africa. In this movie, the last surviving team is called “Gladers” who are immune to the Flare virus that has ravaged the world's population. Gladers eventually escapes the maze and somehow makes it out. WCKD (an organization responsible for capturing end experimenting on immunes) goes after them by sending a drone team. While escaping they have to go through big robotics creatures who were controlled by WCKD.
